Discrimination Against Individuals with Disabilities in Omaha, NE

Overview:
In Omaha, NE, there are various levels of discrimination against individuals with disabilities. This ranges from barriers to work to ableistic structural designs to outright segregation. My research on this topic was designed to encompass the whole effects of ableistic discrimination on the local level of Omaha, NE, and to find ways in which we can improve our society for all.

In this website, you will find one agency in Omaha, NE, that combats the short-term effects of this issue, related public policies, references in scripture and other documents, and a works cited page.
